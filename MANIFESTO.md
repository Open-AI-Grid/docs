# Why Open AI Grid Exists

- AI should not be available only to those who can afford it.
- AI compute should not be concentrated in a few hyperscale datacenters.
- Millions of CPUs and GPUs sit idle every day.
- Humanity can build a distributed intelligence network.
- Open AI Grid exists to make that possible.

## Our Vision

Open AI Grid aims to create a global network where individuals, communities and organizations can contribute compute resources and access artificial intelligence without centralized control.

Just as the Internet democratized access to information, Open AI Grid aims to democratize access to intelligence.
